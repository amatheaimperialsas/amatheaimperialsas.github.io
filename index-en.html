About Us

AMATHEA IMPERIAL S.A.S. is a company dedicated to offering short-stay accommodation services with the highest standards of quality, attention, and professionalism.

We have two luxury apartments, completely new, furnished, and equipped to meet the needs of those seeking comfort, privacy, and style during their stay.

We are known for our kindness, discretion, and commitment to our guests. Our goal is to make every visit a pleasant and memorable experience, offering ideal environments for rest, work, or tourism.

We believe hospitality is an art, and that is why we work every day to make every person who chooses us feel welcome, cared for, and valued.
