About Us


AMATHEA IMPERIAL S.A.S. is a company dedicated to offering short-stay accommodation services with the highest standards of quality, attention, and professionalism. We were born with the vision of providing each guest with an experience that combines the comfort of home with the excellence of personalized hotel service. We have two luxury apartments, completely new, furnished, and equipped to meet the needs of those seeking comfort, privacy, and style during their stay. Each space has been carefully designed with attention to detail, prioritizing elegance, functionality, and security. At AMATHEA IMPERIAL S.A.S., we are known for our kindness, discretion, and commitment to our guests. Our goal is to ensure that each visit is a pleasant, comfortable, and memorable experience, offering ideal environments for rest, work, or tourism. We believe hospitality is an art, and that is why we work every day to make every person who chooses us feel welcome, cared for, and valued.
